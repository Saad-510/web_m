CHIKEN FRIED RICE
This chicken fried rice recipe is very easy and tasty! It's basically cooked
chicken, rice, soy sauce, and shredded egg stir-fried together. For best resu
use leftover cooked rice.

INGREDIENTS;

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

step 1;

Gather all INGREDIENTS;

step 2;

Beat egg and water together in small bowl. Melt butter in a large skillet or
wok over medium-low heat; add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes.
Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds; set asidean overhead shot of egg cooked in a skillet

step 3;

Heat oil in the same skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes;
stir in shredded egg. Serve hot.
